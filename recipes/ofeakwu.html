Ofeakwu

Description

Ofeakwu is a nutritious sauce/stew prepared from the extraction of palm oil from palm fruit.

Ingredients

  • Palm fruit
  • Onions
  • Fish
  • Meat
  • Scent leaf
  • Dried fish
  • Crayfish
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Ugba
  • Ogiri
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Boil the meat and fish for thirty minutes - adding garlic and ginger.
  2. Wash and cook the palm fruits for an hour and half.
  3. Pound and extract the oil and fluid from the palm fruit.
  4. Boil the extracted fluid for another thirty minutes before adding the fish and meat.
  5. Add ugba, ogiri, onions, pepper, and grounded crayfish.
  6. Wait for ten minutes and add your salt and chopped utazi leafs.
